Registrum

About

Registrum (which is Latin for the noun register) is a unique app to help remember and organise things: what to do, what to buy, new ideas, ... but with a difference - simplicity and flexibility! Because of its intuitive design and simple, yet powerful, features, this app is almost guaranteed to become a best friend, used daily. Choose the LITE version to familiarise yourself with its basic functionality, and upgrade to the (almost free) FULL version when you have discovered how useful it is. It will be your best investment EVER!

Main Features

  • YOU are always in control: create your own customised "Registrum lists", use your own words, voice, expressions, emojis, ... and organise things the way YOU want it
  • It is easy-to-use with a nice-looking interface
  • It has a simple and intuitive design with extensive help descriptions
  • Create unlimited number of Active and Historical lists
  • Use voice recording and/or language-independent voice-to-text entry
  • Add emojis to give it a personal look that is colourful and attractive
  • Forget prefined lists or icons that you don't want anyway
  • Use it in locked mode for easy one-hand operation while, for example, shopping
  • Let someone add items to your list remotely using WhatsApp or Telegram
  • Send a Registrum list to other Registrum users
  • Create private lists that only YOU can access
  • ... and much, much more ...
